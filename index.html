Methodology & Data Transparency

What this dashboard shows: Anonymised, aggregate threat patterns detected by on-device AI across opted-in families. No individual student data is ever visible.

What flows from the device: Alert category (grooming, bullying, etc.), severity level (low/medium/high/critical), timestamp, and age bracket. Never message content, student names, or device identifiers.

Confidence levels: Data confidence depends on adoption rate. Below 30% adoption, patterns may not be statistically representative. We label confidence on every metric.

Detection accuracy: Published quarterly. Current: English 88% precision, Danish 84% precision. False positive rate: ~8%. We are transparent about limitations.

Consent: Parents opt in to school mode during app onboarding. They can opt out at any time. Without opt-in, their child's data is not included in this dashboard.

Standard: Custorian Controls Framework v0.1 · IEEE P3462 aligned · custorian.org
